I've honestly been putting in around 70 hours into my learning every single week. And, you know what... it's paying off. I can not develop web applications. ANd, for once, I'm really starting to see that this is another amazing set of skills I can add to my "toolbag of life" and use to build a meaningful future for myself and my family.
